Project D Team Structure v6.0

Version 6.0 May 2026 Confidential Internal Blueprint

Dedicated Agent: Operations & Capacity Agent (Ops-Agent)

Monitors task lists and delivery speeds. When a lead is moved to Closed Won on our own CRM board, the Ops-Agent automatically spins up a new client sandbox database and opens a shared communications channel.

Level 01: Executive Leadership

Founder & CEO

1 Headcount

The Visionary Architect. Leads corporate strategy, secures high-value partnerships, and closes Enterprise contracts by selling the power of our agentic CRM.

  • CRM Sandbox Demo: Opens the AMP Center live demo and walks prospects through simulated workflows to demonstrate live visual organization.
  • Lead Dossiers: Reviews prospects' detailed interaction logs, compiled automatically by outreach agents in the lead files.
AMP Center Integration: Simulator Console (runs live automation scripts to demo client workflows to close deals).

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

1 Headcount

The Integrator. Coordinates internal operations and manages Tech and Creative resource allocations via our agentic pipelines.

  • Stats Tracking: Audits dashboard Stats Cards (Total Active Leads, Saved Hours, active routes) to gauge agency capacity.
  • Logs Review: Monitors the AMP Center system log panel to track team completion times and identify bottlenecks.
AMP Center Integration: System Logs & Stats Panel (monitors team velocity metrics and agent execution logs).

Level 02: Creative Production

Creative Production Lead

1 Headcount

The Visual Architect. Creates 3D visuals, logos, CGI campaigns, and conversion-centric website wireframes, managing assets directly in the database.

  • Asset Integration: Syncs design assets to the Media Agent inside the AMP Center to automatically scale image delivery.
  • SMS Template Assets: Links visual elements directly to outbound campaign cards inside the CRM UI.
AMP Center Integration: Media Library & File Manager (syncs visual assets directly to booking pages and marketing slots).

Level 03: Technology & Engineering

Lead App & Web Developer

1 Headcount

The Technical Backbone. Builds CRM database schema, web hooks, APIs, and manages the AMP Center server environments.

  • Webhook & Agent Configuration: Uses the AMP Center sandbox developer environment to configure response triggers and link specialized agents.
  • Workflow Engineering: Codes modular actions that allow the CRM UI to properly organize and direct agent activities.
AMP Center Integration: API Manager & Webhook Triggers (manages pipeline logic and custom integrations).

Jr. App & Web Developer

1 Headcount

The Implementation Engine. Supports the Lead Developer in setting up client databases, configuring forms, and testing agent connection routes.

  • Widget Setup: Embeds booking calendars and review sliders into client web domains linked to active CRM boards.
  • Kanban Cards Formatting: Configures Kanban stages, lead tags, and audits database security tokens.
AMP Center Integration: Client Database Manager (provisions customer pipelines and assigns agent slots).

Level 04: Growth & Success

Sales Development Rep (SDR)

2 Headcount

Processes leads, schedules calls, and moves candidates through the pipeline, relying on AMP's auto-outreach.

  • Uses **Leads Board** to monitor automated text/email conversations organized by the outreach agent.
  • Reviews **Lead Details Drawer** logs to check engagement histories before jumping on calls.

Client Success Manager

2 Headcount

Manages client lifecycles, checks system usage, and coordinates onboarding transitions.

  • Monitors active campaigns and client usage metrics via the CRM dashboard.
  • Intervenes when the **Sentiment Agent** flags a Google review score drop below 3 stars, triggering an alert.

Head of Paid Media

1 Headcount

Designs ad strategies and tracks campaign lead-flow volumes directly into the CRM.

  • Wires web inquiry lead forms to the AMP Center's lead creation webhook.
  • Audits lead-source stats cards to calculate acquisition cost and ROI automatically.

Level 05: Coaching & Support

AI Staff Trainer

1 Headcount

The Onboarding Coach. Leads workshops to ensure client teams master their dashboard capabilities and agent configurations.

  • Simulator Training: Uses the AI Automations Simulator to show client staff how texts trigger automations in their CRM UI.
  • Adoption Metrics: Verifies staff log-in counts inside the database log history to prevent churn.
AMP Center Integration: AI Automations Simulator Console & Logs (reviews workflow simulation results).

Ops & Finance Coordinator

1 Headcount

The Backend Backbone. Oversees contract signings, invoicing, and vendor payouts, utilizing automated triggers.

  • Payment Triggers: Audits transactions processed via checkout hooks and logged in the Finance ledger.
  • Contract Validation: Verifies digital signature completions inside the Lead Drawer.
AMP Center Integration: Finance Module & Transaction Ledger (logs accounts receivable/payable).
