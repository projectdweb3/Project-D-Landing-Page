Project D Team Structure v6.0

Version 6.0 May 2026 Confidential Internal Blueprint

Dedicated Agent: Operations & Capacity Agent (Ops-Agent)

Monitors task lists and delivery speeds. When a lead is moved to Closed Won on our own CRM board, the Ops-Agent automatically spins up a new client sandbox database and opens a shared communications channel.

Level 01: Executive Leadership

Founder & CEO

1 Headcount | Primary Channel: Neural Chat & Strategic Planning

The Visionary Architect. Leads corporate strategy and closes Enterprise contracts. Utilizes Neural Chat to orchestrate company sub-agents and uses Strategic Planning to build custom customer blueprints.

  • Neural Chat Orchestration: Directly instructs and delegates tasks to company sub-agents via the chat console.
  • Strategic Planning Blueprints: Drafts high-level roadmaps and executes live simulations to close new client deals.
AMP Center Ownership: Neural Chat & Strategic Planning

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

1 Headcount | Primary Channel: Settings & Team Chat

The Integrator. Coordinates internal operations and manages resource allocations. Uses Settings to manage permissions and uses Team Chat to align the agency.

  • Settings Oversight: Audits active user accounts, updates business profile parameters, and coordinates configuration.
  • Team Chat Coordination: Monitors internal team channels to unblock deliverables and align project teams.
AMP Center Ownership: Settings (Ops Control) & Team Chat

Level 02: Creative Production

Creative Production Lead

1 Headcount | Primary Channel: Content Factory

The Visual Architect. Creates 3D visuals, logos, CGI campaigns, and conversion-centric website wireframes, managing all media files directly in the Content Factory.

  • Content Factory Library: Manages the media library, indexing CGI drafts and visual elements for active campaigns.
  • Asset Generation: Leverages built-in generation prompts to prototype and refine assets directly in the workspace.
AMP Center Ownership: Content Factory (Media & Asset Manager)

Level 03: Technology & Engineering

Lead App & Web Developer

1 Headcount | Primary Channel: Settings (Developer Options)

The Technical Backbone. Builds database schema, web hooks, APIs, and manages Twilio/email developer integrations inside Settings.

  • API Gateways Configuration: Manages API connection tokens, custom webhooks, and configures sandbox routes.
  • Workflow Engineering: Programmatically configures database trigger logic to synchronize background sub-agents.
AMP Center Ownership: Settings (Developer Credentials)

Jr. App & Web Developer

1 Headcount | Primary Channel: Settings & Tactical Calendar

The Implementation Engine. Provisions client sandboxes in Settings and configures customer scheduling interfaces on the Tactical Calendar.

  • Sandbox Provisioning: Uses templates inside Settings to set up fresh workspaces for onboarding clients.
  • Tactical Calendar Integration: Plugs appointment calendars and webhook triggers directly into client websites.
AMP Center Ownership: Settings (User Provisioning) & Tactical Calendar

Level 04: Growth & Success

Sales Development Rep (SDR)

2 Headcount | Primary: User Pipeline & Gmail Center

Processes leads, tracks booking progress on the User Pipeline, and manages correspondence inside the Gmail Center.

  • Uses the Kanban User Pipeline to update sales stages (New Leads -> Closed Won).
  • Runs email sequences and tracks manual follow-up replies in the Gmail Center.

Client Success Manager (CSM)

2 Headcount | Primary: Subscriber Ledger & Team Chat

Manages subscriber accounts, monitors project delivery, and coordinates communication via Team Chat.

  • Monitors account records, updates data fields, and flags drop-offs in the Subscriber Ledger.
  • Uses Team Chat channels to collaborate on customer briefs and resolve questions.

Head of Paid Media

1 Headcount | Primary: User Pipeline & MRR Tracker

Injects inbound leads into the User Pipeline and audits marketing ROI margins using the MRR Tracker.

  • Wires lead form webhook integrations to feed directly into the User Pipeline.
  • Audits campaign cost metrics against subscription valuations on the MRR Tracker dashboard.

Level 05: Coaching & Support

AI Staff Trainer

1 Headcount | Primary: Neural Chat & Tactical Calendar

The Onboarding Coach. Teaches client teams to interact with Neural Chat and schedules workshops on the Tactical Calendar.

  • Neural Chat Coaching: Certifies staff members on utilizing Neural Chat to query data and invoke custom actions.
  • Webinar Scheduling: Coordinates onboarding calls and group training blocks on the Tactical Calendar.
AMP Center Ownership: Neural Chat (Simulator Console) & Tactical Calendar

Ops & Finance Coordinator

1 Headcount | Primary Channel: MRR Tracker

The Backend Backbone. Manages subscriber contracts, processes invoicing, and monitors transaction logs via the MRR Tracker.

  • MRR Ledger Audits: Reviews customer subscription plans, active MRR totals, and processes manual billing updates.
  • Financial Actions: Manages checkout gateway responses and handles billing disputes in the ledger system.
AMP Center Ownership: MRR Tracker (Financial Ledger)
