The Core Vision

Project D is an AI-first agency built on top of the AMP Center platform. Unlike standard businesses that rely on scattered tools (such as Antigravity, Cursor, or Claude Code) or generic chat templates, we run our entire company using our own unified software. The AMP Center acts as our primary assistant agentic workforce, combining autonomous workflows with a robust CRM UI. This offers an organized, structured workspace that the big players in the market simply do not provide today.

AMP-Driven Operations: By combining agentic automation with clean CRM categorization, we eliminate manual lead entry, speed up response times to under 60 seconds, and track candidate onboarding logs. We prove the power of agentic workforces by running our own agency on the very platform we deliver to our clients.

The Two Revenue Pillars

Our business leverages gateway services to onboard high-value clients onto the AMP Center, maximizing customer value and software adoption:

Pillar 1: Gateway Growth Services

High-ROI design, development, and animation services (AI-powered website makeovers, custom applications, and 3D graphics) sold directly to businesses. These services act as the primary client acquisition gateway.

  • High-Impact Assets: Premium design and custom builds that instantly wow prospects.
  • Onboarding Hook: Client deliverables are pre-integrated to work natively inside the AMP Center dashboard.
  • Value Realization: Establishes design authority and trust, making software onboarding natural.

Pillar 2: Premium AMP Center Onboarding

Onboarding active service clients into the AMP Center—our proprietary CRM dashboard and custom agent workforce software. While self-serve users get standard access, our premium clients get hands-on co-engineering and training.

  • Co-Managed Dashboards: Full 24/7 access to the Project D development team for custom integrations and support.
  • On-Site Staff Training: We visit client businesses to train their team hands-on, maximizing their operational profits.
  • High-Ticket Software: Captures premium recurring subscription and maintenance revenue.
