The Core Vision

Project D is an AI-first agency built on top of the **AMP Center** platform. Unlike standard businesses that rely on scattered tools (such as Antigravity, Cursor, or Claude Code) or generic chat templates, we run our entire company using our own unified software. The AMP Center acts as our primary **assistant agentic workforce**, combining autonomous workflows with a robust CRM UI. This offers an organized, structured workspace that the big players in the market simply do not provide today.

AMP-Driven Operations: By combining agentic automation with clean CRM categorization, we eliminate manual lead entry, speed up response times to under 60 seconds, and track candidate onboarding logs. We prove the power of agentic workforces by running our own agency on the very platform we deliver to our clients.

The Two Revenue Pillars

Our business relies on the AMP Center platform to monetize and support two distinct customer segments, centered around deploying an organized agentic workforce:

Pillar 1: Flagship SaaS Platform

A self-serve, hands-off platform accessible to any business. Customers subscribe, pick specialized agent templates, and utilize pre-configured CRM workflows to automate their operations.

  • Automated Provisioning: Sign-ups instantly spin up client databases.
  • Self-Guided Tutorials: Built-in guides walk users through connecting phone and email gateways to their new agents.
  • Hands-off Income: High-margin SaaS recurring revenue stream with zero service overhead.

Pillar 2: Enterprise Integrations

High-ticket consulting and custom co-engineering services. We build bespoke agentic workflows, script complex APIs, and train client teams to leverage the AMP Center's full capability.

  • Custom Agent Sandboxes: Bespoke dashboards engineered specifically for the client's operations.
  • Direct Co-Engineering: Direct development support integrating legacy databases and platforms.
  • Staff Certifications: Monthly coaching to ensure high platform adoption and employee mastery of the agentic workforce.

The 4-Phase Ecosystem

We co-engineer client success using the AMP Center as the foundation of every build:

Phase 01
The Discovery Blueprint

Real-Time Architecture. The CEO and Lead Dev present a live AMP Center sandbox. We map the client's current workflows and configure lead triggers live during the call, demonstrating the instant organization of the CRM UI.

Phase 02
Service Selection & Strategy

We define the specific CRM widgets, custom SMS hooks, and 3D visual templates needed for the client's custom agentic database.

Phase 03
Rapid Implementation

We hard-code the CRM forms into the client's web domains and test webhook triggers (like POS checkouts triggering SMS reviews).

Phase 04
Operational Home Base

The client's dashboard is fully launched. Staff members log in, complete initial tutorials, and manage their automated agentic workforce via the Kanban Board.
