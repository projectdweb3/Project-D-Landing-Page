Strategic Monetization Blueprint

Project D has structured its monetization engine to capture value from two separate market segments: self-serve users globally who need automated assistant workforces (Hands-Off SaaS Tiers), and high-growth businesses who need custom agentic workflow engineering integrated with visual organizational tools (High-Touch Enterprise Tiers). Our core value proposition is that we provide a fully organized CRM UI wrapper around complex agentic behaviors—something standard LLM chat interfaces cannot match.

1. Personal SaaS Tiers (Self-Serve AMP Center)

A self-serve subscription system. Users connect their own Twilio/email gateways and use the standard AMP Center interface to organize contacts, trigger workflows, and manage their automated agentic assistants.
