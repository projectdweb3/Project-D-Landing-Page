1. Aesthetic Authority

The Creative Production Lead is the visual architect of Project D. In the AI era, attention is expensive, and visual dominance is the only way to capture it. Your mission is to create assets that feel "too good to be true" yet function with clinical conversion precision, working hand-in-hand with our AMP Center agentic workflows.

The Creative Philosophy: We don't just "design." We engineer authority. Every asset—from a 3D logo to a mobile app UI—must feel premium, futuristic, and indispensable, seamlessly integrated into our CRM system.

The Creative Stack

Everything you do is powered by the most advanced creative tools available:

  • Higgsfield AI (Primary): Your central hub for logo creation, graphic design, and AI-driven animation. Membership access is provided for all project work.
  • Supporting Tools: Utilize Canva, Photoshop, and Illustrator for fine-tuning layout design, vectors, and typography details.

2. The Production Workflow (Content Factory)

You do not work in a vacuum. Your primary operational workspace is the Content Factory inside the AMP Center, where your visual assets are generated, indexed, and deployed to serve our sales and client pipelines.

  • Content Factory Uploads: When visual assets or ad designs are finished, upload and categorize them directly within the Content Factory Media Library. This makes them immediately available to active campaigns.
  • Asset Library Syncing: Your assets are automatically resized and indexed by the Media Agent in Content Factory, mapping them to specific leads in the CRM database.
  • Briefs & Feedback: Monitor task assignments in Content Factory to pull client visual briefs generated by CSMs, translating operational feedback into graphic iterations.
  • AI-Accelerated Prototyping: Use integrated prompts inside the Content Factory to generate initial mockups, combining Midjourney or Gen-3 models to speed up visual brainstorming.

3. Core Creative Deliverables

We deliver two primary classes of visual assets, cataloged and managed in the AMP Center:

3D Animation & CGI

Create 3D assets that give clients instant "Category of One" status. CGI stories should be high-impact, short-form, and built for social media dominance, optimized for automated booking funnels.

Website & App UI/UX

Websites are not just static pages; they are automated landing machines. App UIs must be clean, fast, and functional, pairing beauty with CRM lead capture widgets.

4. Design Tokens & Visual Language

Maintain the Project D Aesthetic across every client project:

  • Typography: Bold, clean sans-serifs (Inter, Outfit, Roboto).
  • Grid Systems: Use subtle grid backgrounds and clean white space to evoke a "blueprint" feel.
  • Motion: Smooth, deliberate transitions. No clunky animations.
  • Color: Curated palettes that feel modern and balanced (Teals, Oranges, Sleek Dark Modes).
