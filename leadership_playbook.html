3. The 5-Phase "Wow" Discovery Workflow

The Discovery Call is a live demonstration of speed, design authority, and business automation, using a fresh AMP Center sandbox configured for the prospect.

Phase 1: Deep-Dive (Min 0-15)
CEO leads. Ask questions that expose bottlenecks.

Uncover manual repetitions. Identify trigger points like driver callouts (DSP) or delayed booking responses (Med Spa).

CEO: "When a driver calls out sick at 6:00 AM, what is the manual workflow to fill that route?"
Prospect: "Our dispatcher starts texting standby drivers one by one. It usually takes 2 hours and we lose routes."

Phase 2: The Silent Build (Min 5-20)
Lead Developer works silently.

While the CEO continues discussing business bottlenecks, the Developer uses the AMP Center sandbox developer environment to spin up a new database and scaffold a 3D-heavy conversion page containing the prospect's logo and branding.

Phase 3: The "Wow" Reveal (Min 20-25)
The Screen Shift.

The CEO instructs the Developer to share their screen. Present the live dashboard sandbox and landing page. This proves that we can build in 15 minutes what other agencies pitch in 3 weeks, using the organized UI of the AMP Center.

Phase 4: Service Fitting (Min 25-30)
Interactive Simulation.

The CEO directs the prospect to the AI Automations tab in their sandbox. Click "Run Trigger" (e.g. 6 AM Standby Broadcast). Let them watch the logs output in real-time and see the mock phone screen receive and accept the standby route alert, demonstrating how their new agentic workforce stays organized.

CEO: "As you can see on the simulator, the minute a sick report is logged, the CRM queries the active standby list, broadcasts a text, and assigns the claim in 60 seconds on autopilot, keeping everything structured on your board."

Phase 5: Closing to Blueprint
Secure commitment to Phase 01.

We do not close a full year contract on this call. We close the commitment to the Discovery Blueprint (Phase 01) to map their specific system architecture and prepare their sandbox.

4. The COO Integrator's Mission

The COO runs the operational backend of Project D across three dimensions, utilizing the AMP Center's Settings and Team Chat channels to maintain order:

1. Workspace Control (Settings Channel)

Ensure the team has appropriate database permissions. Manage user seats, register API gateways, and configure sub-agent priorities in the Settings tab.

2. Flow Management (Team Chat Channel)

Coordinate project channels in Team Chat. Direct the developers, creative leads, and CSMs to execute client delivery without communication friction.

3. Dashboard Mastery

Audits dashboard Stats Cards and system logs to track task resolution times, ensuring bottlenecks are cleared before they impact operations.

"The Integrator's Tone: You are the voice of Systemic Order. When the agency scales fast, you ensure the quality of delivery never dips."
