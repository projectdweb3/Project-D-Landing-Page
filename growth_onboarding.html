Dedicated Agents: Lead Outreach Agent (SDR-Agent) & Customer Sentiment Agent (CSM-Agent)

The SDR-Agent scrapes local business databases for green flags (e.g., slow Google response times) and automates personalized outreach scripts. The CSM-Agent monitors client CRM activity, logs satisfaction ratings, and automatically triggers feedback briefs for the Creative Lead.

Role 01: Head of Paid Media

You drive the high-intent interest that fuels the agency. You are not just a media buyer—you are a Pipeline Architect using our agentic CRM to manage and organize lead-flow. Your primary workspaces are the User Pipeline (to track lead source injections) and the MRR Tracker (to audit client value vs ad spend).

Core Brand Standards

  • Visual Authority: Every ad must look like it cost $50k to produce. If it doesn't match our 3D/CGI visual standard, it doesn't go live.
  • Quality Over Volume: Your goal is not "cheap clicks." It's qualified problems. Target prospects who have the issues our agentic workforce solves.

AMP Center Execution Pointers

  • API Lead Injection (User Pipeline): Wire all Facebook Lead Forms and Google Ads landing page forms directly to the AMP Center's lead creation webhook. Map lead parameters (like name, email, phone, and campaign source) so our Outreach agents get instant triggers.
  • Feed the Outreach Agents: Provide problem context. Ensure the incoming lead payload logs the specific pain point (e.g., "Clicked Botox 90-day Recall Ad") so the automated outreach agent knows what script templates to run.
  • MRR Tracker Audits: Cross-reference lead acquisition costs with active client subscription plans in the MRR Tracker to calculate real-time client acquisition efficiency.

Role 02: Sales Development Rep (SDR)

You move prospects from Chaos to Blueprint. You are selling a free strategy session, not a contract. Your work is supercharged by the AMP Center's built-in agents, allowing you to manage 5x more prospects with zero clutter. Your primary workspaces are the User Pipeline (managing the Leads Board) and the Gmail Center (syncing email communications).

How to Navigate the AMP Center CRM

  • The Leads Board (User Pipeline): Your primary workspace is the Kanban board inside User Pipeline. Manage your workflow by dragging cards through the pipeline: New Leads → Contacted → Demo Scheduled → Trial Active → Closed Won.
  • Audit the Lead Drawer: Click on a lead card to open the Lead Details Drawer. Before placing a call or sending a manual message, review the Automation Activity Log. Check what automated messages the outreach agent has already sent (e.g., "10:45 AM - Response SMS triggered") and see if they clicked the booking link. Leverage this visual organization to frame your conversation.
  • Gmail Center Synchronization: Connect your personal inbox, track cold email delivery metrics, and reply to high-intent responses directly within the Gmail Center tab.

The Golden Rule: If they want anything we offer—a website makeover, a 3D animation, or a CRM sandbox—it is a win. Don't force the full program. Book the Discovery Call first, where we will show them the power of an organized CRM-integrated agentic workforce.

Role 03: Client Success Manager (CSM)

You are the Face of Project D post-sale. Your goal is retention and onboarding success by ensuring client teams adopt and master their new CRM-integrated agentic workforce. Your primary workspaces are the Subscriber Ledger (managing active clients) and Team Chat (managing project coordination).

Core Brand Standards

  • Partnership, Not Support: You don't "fix tickets." You optimize outcomes. Your tone is one of confidence and expertise.
  • Proactive Communication: If a client hasn't heard from you in a week, you've failed. You are the heartbeat of their growth.

AMP Center Execution Pointers

  • Subscriber Ledger Oversight: Actively audit the Subscriber Ledger to log client feedback notes, update service tiers, and monitor customer lifetime value metrics.
  • Monitor Sentiment Agent Triggers: In the Google Reviews Booster workflow, when a client logs a customer satisfaction rating of <= 3 stars, the AMP Center automatically triggers a Manager Alert notification to your dashboard. You must call that client within 1 hour to resolve the friction point.
  • The Bridge (Team Chat): Turn client feedback into Creative Briefs. Add new tasks and feature requests directly into internal Team Chat project threads so the Creative Production Lead has clear guidelines.
  • Proficiency Audits: Review the client's staff active login metrics in the dashboard database. If their weekly active logins drop, schedule a follow-up coaching review.
