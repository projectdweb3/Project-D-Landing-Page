Your 4-Step Deployment Roadmap

Once you sign on, our teams proceed along this timeline to deploy your custom agentic database sandbox:

Step 01
Formalize the Partnership

We execute a digital signature on the engagement agreement. Upon signing, your private AMP Center database is automatically provisioned and populated with pre-configured agent templates (e.g. Botox Reactivation or Onboarding Portal).

Action Required: Sign the AMP Program Engagement Agreement sent to your email.
Step 02
Infrastructure Integration

Our Technology team configures your Twilio SMS gateway, connects email endpoints, maps web inquiry hooks, and installs booking calendars on your domains. This links your public channels directly to the CRM **Leads Board**, allowing your new agents to start logging and categorizing conversations.

Outcome: A live, functional CRM database sandbox linked to your website and phone lines.
Step 03
Creative & Growth Dominance

While technology is being wired up, our Creative Lead uploads your custom 3D animations and brand assets to the AMP file manager. Simultaneously, our Paid Media head wires lead-capture forms to your lead creation webhooks.

Outcome: Ad campaigns driving leads directly into your **New Leads** Kanban column, triggering automatic agent nurtures.
Step 04
Staff Proficiency Training

Our AI Staff Trainer hosts an interactive workshop with your team, using the **AI Automations Simulator** to test keyword response messages, review booster triggers, and certify staff members on how to manage their new agentic workforce.

Action Required: Book your first 60-minute staff training session via our scheduling calendar.
