Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Overview

Project D ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website projectd.io and the AMP Center platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including our Google OAuth integration and Gmail features.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by your identity provider.

Google OAuth Data

If you sign in with Google, we receive an OAuth token that allows you to authenticate. We store a refresh token securely in our database to maintain your session and, if you grant Gmail access, to access your Gmail on your behalf.

Gmail Data

When you connect your Gmail account (either through Google sign-in or the Settings page), we request access to read, send, and manage your emails (gmail.readonly, gmail.send, gmail.modify). Your email content is fetched in real-time when you use the Gmail Center and is never stored on our servers. We only store your OAuth refresh token and connected email address.

Business Data

Information you enter in the AMP Center (business profile, leads, clients, tasks, calendar events, inventory) is stored in your account to provide the service and sync across your devices.

How We Use Your Information

  • • Authentication: To create and manage your account, verify your identity, and maintain your session across devices.
  • • Gmail Integration: To display your inbox, send emails on your behalf, and allow AI-assisted email drafting — only when you actively use the Gmail Center feature.
  • • Cross-Device Sync: To synchronize your business data (leads, tasks, calendar, etc.) across all your devices.
  • • Service Improvement: To monitor platform performance and improve the user experience.

Data Security & Storage

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • • OAuth tokens are stored securely in our Supabase database with row-level security policies.
  • • Email content is never cached or stored — it is fetched from Gmail's API in real-time and displayed only in your browser.
  • • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • • Google OAuth / Gmail API: For authentication and email features. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • • Supabase: For database storage and authentication infrastructure.
  • • Netlify: For hosting and serverless functions.
  • • OpenAI: For AI-powered features. Prompts may include business context you provide but never raw email content unless you explicitly ask the AI to draft a reply.

Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure

Project D's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Your Rights & Controls

  • • Disconnect Gmail: You can disconnect your Gmail at any time from Settings. This immediately deletes your stored refresh token.
  • • Revoke Access: You can revoke Project D's access to your Google account at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • • Delete Account: You can reset all your data from Settings > Danger Zone, which permanently removes all stored business data.
  • • Data Export: Contact us to request a copy of your stored data.
